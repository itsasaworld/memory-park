Navigation Guide

Movement Controls:

• ↑ — Walk forward
• ← — Move left
• → — Move right
• Click and drag — Step backward and look around

Camera Controls:

• Click and drag — Look freely around you
• Shift + ↑ / ↓ — Tilt view up or down (like moving your head)

Memory Submission:

• Images must be in JPG or PNG format
• HEIC format is not supported
